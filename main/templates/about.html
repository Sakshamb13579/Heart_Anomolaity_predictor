Who are WE ?
The incidence of heart-related diseases has been increasing at an alarming rate in recent years ,especially aggravated with the onset of COVID-19 .According to WHO, cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of death globally.
This can be attributed to a variety of factors, including the ongoing pandemic and its related side effects , unhealthy diets etc.The increasing trend of heart-related diseases requires a multi-faceted approach, including the promotion of healthy lifestyles, early detection with management of risk factors, and access to effective treatments.
In conclusion, the rising trend of heart-related illnesses is a call for action to prevent and control these diseases. By taking a proactive approach to heart health, we can help to reduce the impact of heart disease and improve the health and well-being of individuals and communities around the world.
